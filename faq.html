Frequently Asked Questions

Q. What is YT-SUB-APP?

YT-SUB-APP is a promotion platform that helps you gain engagement by reaching more users with your channels and videos.

Q. How to use YT-SUB-APP?

You can earn points by watching videos and liking videos. You can create campaigns with these points.

Q. If I delete my campaign, will my points be refunded?

Unused points from deleted campaigns will be automatically refunded to your account balance.

Q. I encountered an error in the app. What should I do?

You can report technical issues to info.ytsubapp53@gmail.com for support and resolution.

Q. Are the views and likes real?

Yes, they are from real users like you who participate in the YT-SUB-APP community to grow their own reach.

Q. Can I use more than one account?

According to our policy, a maximum of 3 accounts per device is allowed. More than that will lead to a permanent ban.
