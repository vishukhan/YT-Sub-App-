1. Purpose of the Policy

This policy explains how personal, device, and security-signal data collected during the use of the ytSocial application is securely processed, stored, used, and, when necessary, shared in accordance with legal regulations.

2. Data Collected

2.1. Device and System Data:
  • Device name, brand, model, and Android version.
  • Hardware ID (HWID), Android ID, and MAC address.
  • IP address and network provider information.
  • Screen resolution and battery status.
2.2. User Account Data:
  • Email address used for Google Sign-In.
  • YouTube channel name, channel ID, and profile picture.
  • Videos watched, likes, and completed subscription tasks.
  • Referral history and coin balance.

3. Use of Data

Data is used to provide, maintain, and improve the application services, detect fraud, and ensure the security of the coin-earning ecosystem.

4. Data Sharing

ytSocial does not sell your personal data. Data may be shared with third-party infrastructure providers (like Firebase) for app functionality or as required by law.

5. Anti-Fraud & Misuse Policy

To maintain a fair ecosystem, the following actions are strictly prohibited:

  • Creating more than 3 accounts from the same device (Multi-accounting).
  • Use of emulators, virtual devices, or modified APKs.
  • Automated bots, clickers, or script usage.
  • Quickly unsubscribing or unliking after earning coins.

Violations will result in permanent account termination and forfeiture of all coins.

6. Data Security

We implement high-level encryption (SSL/TLS) to protect your data. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

7. Retention of Data

We retain your data as long as your account is active. Inactive accounts for more than 12 months may have their data anonymized or deleted.
